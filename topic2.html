RWBY

I have enjoyed this show made by Rooster Teeth. At first I was unsure about it, but once I watched it I found out I enjoyed it.


Jaune Arc is one of my three favorite characters. I know there are people that hate him as a character.
He isn't like the others, he has no ranged weapon, he didn't unlock his semblance (power) until season five,
and he is not as good as everyone else. I like him because he stands out from the rest.

Another character in my three favorites is Ruby Rose. She is one of the main characters from the show.
She is literally the "R" in RWBY. She is another goofball character. She is serious when needed to be. She has
a scythe that turns into a sniper rifle. She has silver eyes, (a powerful ability), and she turns into rose petals.

My last of the three favorites is Qrow Branwen. He is a cool character that was introduced in season three.
His weapon is a large sword that turns into a scythe and a ranged weapon. He turns into a crow and his
